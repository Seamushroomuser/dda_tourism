Top 10 Popular Amusement Parks In India

Wonderla
Wonderla

Wonderla has a chain of stores throughout India, one of which is in Bangalore. This is one of the best amusement parks in India, with over 50 rides guaranteed to get your heart racing. Wonderla, located on the outskirts of Bangalore, is famous for its high-thrill rides that get your heart pumping.




Kingdom of Dreams
Kingdom of Dreams

While Kingdom of Dreams is not your typical theme park with rides and games, it does have its own allure and uniqueness. Its concept is to showcase India’s rich and diverse culture through live entertainment, culinary arts, and a variety of other attractions. Visitors can learn about Indian culture through theatre, Bollywood musicals, street entertainment, and a variety of cuisines.

Imagica Adlabs

This theme park, inspired by Universal Studios, is regarded as one of the best amusement parks in India. Essel World
Essel World

In 1989, Essel World, India’s first full-scale amusement park, opened. It has about 70 rides, a Water Kingdom water park, and other attractions such as an ice skating rink, a bowling alley, and special events during the Holi festival. Della Adventure Park
Della Adventure Park

Della Adventure Park, like Imagica Adlabs, is located in Lonavala, but unlike most theme parks, this one focuses on bringing out your daredevil and adventurous side. Della Adventure Park is regarded as one of the best amusement parks in India.




Science City
Science City

Science City in Kolkata is a one-of-a-kind Science Museum and Theme Park. Science City, a wonderful blend of education and entertainment, is always bustling with students and science enthusiasts eager to study and absorb science’s wonders. Science City is one of the best amusement parks in India, which will surely take your attention if you’re visiting with your family and friends. Nicco Park
Nicco Park

One of the best amusement parks in India, Nicco Park, is also referred to as the Disneyland of Kolkata. Located on the city's eastern fringes, it has a mix of wet and dry rides for people of all ages. Local visitors and people visiting Kolkata love this place and the full dose of entertainment and recreation. Major attractions are Nicco Super Bowl, Bowlers Den New, Wet-O-Wild, and amazing parks. Pool tables, bowling alleys, etc., witness huge crowds. Plan a visit to this park and enjoy riding some of the best rides.




Appu Ghar
Appu Ghar

After the Asian Games ended in 1982, India received its first-ever amusement park in 1984. The famous mascot of Asian Games, 'Appu,' was the inspiration behind this park. It came up in Delhi, but it was shut down in 2008. The park reopened amidst anticipation from thrill-seekers in 2014 in Gurgaon. It houses Skyfall, the tallest ride in the country. The other rides worth exploring are Lazy & Crazy River, OMG, Typhoon Tunnel, Rapid Racer, and more. The amusement park also houses amazing restaurants, serving a variety of delicacies. Some of them are Southern Bay, Langkawi Bay, Aroma Coast, and Tidal Toast.




Ocean Park
Ocean Park

It is spread over 20 acres of landscaped gardens and is a popular weekend gateway for the urban population, being situated just 15 km away from the state capital of Hyderabad. Ocean Park is a lot of water with pots of fun for everyone and is one of the best amusement parks in India.
